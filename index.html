About Luminous Reader

Luminous Reader

Version 1.0.0

A modern, elite reading lamp application that transforms your monitor into a beautiful reading light. Perfect for reading physical books in dark environments without turning on room lights.

Key Features

  • Multiple reading modes (Focus, Relax, Night, Study)
  • Customizable colors and brightness
  • Progressive Web App (PWA) support
  • Responsive design for all devices
  • Keyboard shortcuts
  • Glassmorphism UI design
